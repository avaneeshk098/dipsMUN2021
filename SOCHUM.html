UN Social, Cultural and Humanitarian Council (SOCHUM)

The Social, Humanitarian, and Cultural Committee (SOCHUM) is the Third Committee of the General Assembly of the United Nations. It focuses broadly on the welfare and human rights of protected groups such as refugees, children, and in general victims of discrimination.

At least six hundred thousand people have been forced to evacuate their homes in Afghanistan since 2021, most of whom are from the northern districts. The number of internally displaced people (IDPs) continues to climb, with women and children accounting for 80 percent of this tally. Delegates of the SOCHUM will be called upon to discuss the issues arising from displacement and countermeasures for the same.

90% of women in Afghanistan have faced some form of domestic abuse. Such abhorrent statistics have almost become a mainstay of modern media when talking about Afghanistan. Times have changed and the situation has changed with it. Can this committee come up with a method to solve the problems presented by the religious backwardness of Afghanistan?
