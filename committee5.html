UNITED NATIONS SECURITY COUNCIL

The situation in Western Sahara today is one of widespread, debilitating chaos and anarchy. Peace and stability are but a faraway dream to the people of the region. Daily conflicts and human rights violations plague the region, preventing any semblance of development and safety for the people. Calamitous cruelties by both factions of the conflict have unreservedly destabilized the delicate area, leading to festering mistrust amongst the people against a government that appears nothing short of tyrannical. Blameless citizens are forced to confront unnumbered perils daily, leading to a displacement crisis of an unprecedented degree. Heavily disputed demands for independence by the “Sahrawi Arab Republic” have only added to the seemingly insurmountable challenges being faced by the region. The raging conflicts in the region have induced international indignation and sweeping condemnation of the gross human rights violations by stakeholders in the dispute. As delegates of the world community, you must take it upon yourselves to restabilize the region and realize the peoples’ fading dream of a secure, peaceful home. Delegates welcome to the final committee of DiPSMUN 2020, the United Nations Security Council.
