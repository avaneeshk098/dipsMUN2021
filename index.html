Message from the secretary general

Dear Delegates,

It gives me immense pleasure to welcome you to the tenth edition of DiPSMUN. As we continue to handle a rapidly evolving world, our ability to adapt and form objective, logical opinions on all that we see is assuming more importance every day. It is with this sentiment that I’m excited to be your Secretary-General for this conference.

DiPSMUN has seen humble beginnings and reached incredible heights from there. It started out as a small intra-school event and its latest offline edition in 2019 saw over 550 delegates in attendance from all over the country. It is now a flagship event of DPS South that is eagerly anticipated by students and teachers alike, bringing along a wave of excitement, enthusiasm, and passion. Despite the challenges faced and setbacks suffered, the conference has continued to evolve, taking on new forms and purpose as we, as a global community, have navigated the pandemic.

A principle that I’m a strong proponent of is healthy dissent. The ability to cogently deliver a point of view, engage with opposing debate and separate yourself from a situation to think critically is imperative in an increasingly polarising world. MUNs have often been dismissed as echo chambers for the privileged, but I urge you to think approach this conference the way you would real-world scenarios of similar gravity: come ready to listen, come ready to build, and come ready for plenty of healthy dissent, that leads to real, applicable solutions.

“A speech is about politics, about order, brotherhood, and power. But speeches are for campaigning. Now is the time for action.”
– Rick Sanchez, Rick and Morty

As we continue to build and set new standards of achievement as a conference, I cannot wait for all of you to experience what has been and continues to be one of Bangalore’s most unique learning experiences. The Executive Board and I are looking forward to seeing your best foot forward over the two days.

Pranav S Aatreya
Secretary General, DiPSMUN 2022
