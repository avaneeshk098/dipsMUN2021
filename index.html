Message from the secretary general

Dear Delegates,

It gives me immense pleasure to welcome you to the tenth edition of DiPSMUN. As we continue to fight the COVID-19 pandemic and push for normalcy, the need for alternative platforms of diplomacy and peacekeeping have arisen. Geo-political scenarios have been conferred another dimension and it is crucial that we build a populace that is socially aware of the intricacies surrounding our global state of affairs.

DiPSMUN has seen humble beginnings and reached incredible heights from there. It started out as a small intra-school event and its latest offline edition in 2019 saw over 550 delegates in attendance from all over the country. It is now a flagship event of DPS South that is eagerly anticipated by students and teachers alike, bringing along a wave of excitement, enthusiasm and passion. Despite the challenges faced and setbacks suffered, DiPSMUN has successfully adapted to the use of virtual platforms in continuing its mission and legacy. DiPSMUN 2022 brings to you new committees, explores new frontiers and will feature deliberations as intense as ever.

MUNs face criticism for being performative in nature and utilised as an avenue for organised debauchery, however, the perseverance of DiPSMUN along with other Model UN conferences on online platforms goes to show that these criticisms could not be further from the truth. The first step to inducing change is to know what changes are necessary and how they can be facilitated. Learning to work with your peers, crossing geopolitical hurdles together and putting your point across amidst chaos are requisite skillsets that MUNs have been building for years and its importance cannot be ignored. It is critical that now, more than ever, we acknowledge the state of the world as it is.

“A speech is about politics, about order, brotherhood, and power. But speeches are for campaigning. Now is the time for action.”
– Rick Sanchez, Rick and Morty

The need to address pressing issues that pose a threat to global peace and internal security is greater than ever and it is time for you to step up and take charge. Be prepared for a learning experience unlike any other and be sure to bring your A game delegates, because you are going to need it.

Rohan Kanna
Secretary General, DiPSMUN 2022
