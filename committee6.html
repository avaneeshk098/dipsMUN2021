UN Economic and Social Council (ECOSOC)

'If all the economists were laid end to end, they'd never reach a conclusion.' - George Bernard Shaw

Money is responsible for all of us. It creates livelihoods, it destroys lives. It builds up nations, and forces them to fall back down again. And it is the thing that the Economic and Social Council swears by. Development, culture, progress - these can mean nothing without the economy to back them up.

The volume of this situation only aggravates when the paradigm shifts towards the increasingly important digital currency. The impact of this shift is felt by almost every man and woman that lives on this planet, and this impact extends well beyond the economic front.

Delegates, if the world could be compared to a car, you're about to enter the driver's seat with a hundred other people. Make sure you all brake in time, or in the long run, the car's going to crash and we're all dead.
