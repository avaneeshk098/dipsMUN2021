All India Political Parties Meet (AIPPM)

The only possible idea of India is that of a nation greater than the sum of its parts.' - Shashi Tharoor

Republic of India, the largest democracy in the world takes great pride in its distinct political entities and units bound into one political union. Yet, this federal coexistence is constantly challenged by the ever increasing assertions of influence and dominance of the Centre. These constant skirmishes between the demands of regional autonomy and central dominance threaten to tear the threads of unity that the idea of this nation is hanging by.

To protect this idea, and to resolve the conflicts of the Union, it is important to have a body that makes clear to the various leaders of the various ideologies in this country that their goal should be to build the nation higher, not watch it crumble.

The All India Political Parties Meet pits political goliaths against underdogs controlling important vote banks. Remember, no matter how high the stakes go, the greatest coalition of all is India herself and the task to protect her is upon you.
