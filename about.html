About DiPSMUN

WHAT IS A MUN?

Model United Nations is an academic simulation of the United Nations Organization where students essay the role of delegates from different countries and attempt to solve real-world issues with policies and perspectives of their assigned countries.
Topics of various geographical, social, economical and military concerns serve as the agendas of these committees upon which the delegates have intense debate, negotiation, and pass resolution.

Journey of DPS South

DPS South hosted its first Model United Nations conference in 2013, which was an intra-school event with just two committees simulated. Step into 2020, DiPSMUN is one of the most renowned and discernible names in the Bangalore MUN circuit and is growing in stature with each edition. The conference has evolved over these seven years and has attracted hundreds of delegates from all across the city and country due to its unique committees, fierce level of debate, and pleasant ambiance of learning and knowledge.

OUR SECOND e-MUN
