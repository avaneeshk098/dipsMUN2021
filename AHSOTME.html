Ad Hoc Session on the Middle East

Against the gruesome backdrop of everlasting bloodshed between militias and militaries, oppressive brutal regimes, rampant communal sentiments and a never ending lust for power and land, the Middle East is home to one of the richest cultures and home to places which have tremendous religious significance.

Stark and strong ideological differences coupled with historical skirmishes for lands which havenâ€™t come to a close, has thoroughly lit up this land battered by political instability. The unending Israeli-Palestine crisis, one of the world's most complex and intricate issues, has countless times paved its path for horrendous war crimes, serving as a deterrence for the growth and development of this land. On the other hand the major middle eastern political players, Saudi Arabia and Iran wage countless proxy wars so as to assert their dominance over the land, splitting up the Arabs into factions. States of Syria and Yemen have been completely devastated and torn by war. This political instability, and constant state of war in this region, hasled to the birth of numerous militias and extremist organizations as well as a huge business for global and illegal trade of arms. With various foreign powers being heavily invested in this region, the Middle East remains one of the most crippled geopolitical regions in the world.

Will the region be further pushed into turmoil and chaos? Is the concept of peace and freedom still a utopia? The answer is in your hands, in this Ad Hoc Session of the Middle East.
