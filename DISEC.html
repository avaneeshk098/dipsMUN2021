Disarmament and International Security Committee (DISEC)

The Disarmament and International Security Committee (DISEC) is the first committee of the United Nations General Assembly. This committee addresses and deals with disarmament issues, challenges to global security as well as threats to international peace.

With the introduction of non-state actors, 4GW has introduced new domains of conflict, mixing the lines between politics and power, civilians and combatants. This form of warfare is characterized by its guerilla and terror based tactics. The confusion and chaos created thus poses unique challenges that the DISEC must be ready to combat.

The 5GW is the basis of the information war carried out everyday. The boom in use of social media has also lead to a greater number of users that can be misled with misinformation and censorship. While military leadership may control the borders, public opinion shapes the country's ideals.

Delegates are to assess the current situation and find solutions to combat the rising usage of 4GW and 5GW in conflict zones.
